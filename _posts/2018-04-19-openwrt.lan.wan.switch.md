## 产品开发中，技术评审节点TR的各个阶段：

```shell
root@OpenWrt:~# uci get network.lan.eth_port
lan
root@OpenWrt:~# uci set network.lan.eth_port=wan
root@OpenWrt:~# uci commit
root@OpenWrt:~# /etc/init.d/network restart
```
